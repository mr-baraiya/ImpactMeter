# Results

## Output File
`data/features/player_impact_scores.csv`

## What It Contains
- Player-wise impact timeline
- Rolling impact (`rolling_IM`)
- Recency-weighted impact (`weighted_IM`)
- Final normalized score (`IM_score`)

## Interpretation Guide
- `80+`: high impact
- Around `50`: neutral impact
- `30` and below: lower impact zone

## Visual Validation
Trend plots in `notebooks/impact_model.ipynb` show match-by-match impact movement for selected players.
